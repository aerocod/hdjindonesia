    Geological Services encompass a range of expertise, from geological surveys to site assessments, providing valuable insights for natural resource exploration, environmental analysis, and infrastructure development.

    Learn More

    Technical Studies involve in-depth analyses and evaluations conducted by experts to assess the feasibility, performance, or efficiency of complex systems, processes, or projects, aiding informed decision-making and problem-solving. These studies often cover areas such as engineering, technology, economics, and environmental impact to provide comprehensive insights.

    Learn More

    Mine Planning is a meticulous process that involves designing and optimizing the layout, extraction, and production strategies of mineral resources, ensuring safe and efficient mining operations while maximizing profitability.

    Learn More

    JORC/KCMI JORC (Joint Ore Reserves Committee) and KCMI (Knowledge of the Company, Mineral Industry) are industry standards and frameworks for reporting mineral resources and ore reserves. JORC is widely used in Australia and other countries, while KCMI is a similar standard used in the Philippines. These guidelines ensure transparency and consistency in reporting, providing investors and stakeholders with accurate and reliable information about the mineral resources and reserves of a company or project.

    Learn More

    Operation Management involves overseeing and optimizing an organization's processes and resources to ensure efficient production, delivery of goods/services, and effective utilization of resources to meet business goals. It focuses on planning, coordinating, and controlling various operational activities to enhance productivity, quality, and customer satisfaction while minimizing costs and waste.

    Learn More

    Geotechnical Support provides specialized expertise in assessing and addressing geological and engineering aspects of construction projects, ensuring the stability, safety, and durability of structures and foundations. This includes soil analysis, site investigations, and risk assessments to mitigate potential geotechnical challenges.

    Learn More

    ESG Services ESG services (Environmental, Social, and Governance services) are offerings that assist businesses in evaluating and improving their sustainability practices. These services encompass assessing environmental impact, social responsibility, and governance practices to enhance corporate responsibility, transparency, and long-term value creation. ESG considerations have become crucial for companies seeking to meet ethical standards, comply with regulations, attract investors, and address the concerns of various stakeholders.

    Learn More

    Drone Support Services offer expertise in utilizing unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for various applications. These services encompass drone operations, data capture, aerial surveys, mapping, inspections, and monitoring, providing cost-effective and efficient solutions for industries such as agriculture, construction, infrastructure, environmental monitoring, and more. Drones enable businesses to access real-time visual data and insights, leading to better decision-making, increased safety, and enhanced efficiency in their operations.
